## 课文

```html
Postcard always spoil my holidays. Last summer, I went to Italy. I visited museums and sat in public gardens. Afriedly waiter taught me a few words of Italian. Then he lent me a book. I read a few lines, but I did not understand a word. Every day I thought about postcards. My holidays passed quickly, but I did not send cards to my friends. On the last day I made a big decision. I got up early and I bought thirty-seven cards. I spent the whole day in my room, but I did not write a single card!
```



## 单词

```js
1. send v.(sent sent) 寄，送
	send sb. sth. = send sth. to sb.
	send a card/a message/a letter
2. postcard n. 明信片
3. spoil v. (spoiled or spoilt) 使索然无味，损坏
	- Postcard always spoil my holidays
    - 1. damage v. 可修复的损坏
	- 2. destroy (大范围的)彻底的坏了
    - 3. ruin (慢慢地、一点点)彻底的坏了
4. pubilc adj 公共的
	- public gardens/schools/opinions
	- n.公众、大众
	  The museum is open to the public on sundays.
    - private adj.私人的
5. friendly adj.友好的
	- a friendly waiter
      He is friendly to all of us.
    - lovely lonely timely brotherly(名词加ly变adv.)
6. waiter n.男服务员
	- waitress n.女服务员
7. lend v.(lent lent)借给
	- lend sb. sth. = lend sth. to sb.
      lend me a book = lend a book to me
      Can you lend me 200 dollars?
    - lend v.借入/borrow 借出
      borrow sth. (from sb.)
8. decision n.决定
	It is my final decision
    I made a big decision.
    - decide v. 决定
		decide to do sth.
        I decided to go abroad for further education.
9.whole adj.整个的
10.single adj.唯一的、单一的
```



## 课文语法

```js
【Key structure:】
- 一般过去时
- 主谓双宾
```

```js
【一般过去时:】
- 形式：v.过去式(did)
- 用法：Last summer I went to Italy.
☆ 有过去的时间、上下文有过去的动作
	She had a boyfriend.
	Tom was a student.
- 一般过去变否定疑问
 1. You were happy.
    You were not happy.
    Were you happy?
    How were you?
 2. They had a meeting.
    They did not have a meeting.
    Did they have a meeting?
    What did they have?
```

```html
【课文内容】
1. teach sb sth = teach sth to sb (taught)
2. lend sb. sth. = lend sth. to sb. (lent)
3. read read read
4. think thought thought
	think about
5. pass v.经过
6. send sb. sth. = send sth. to sb.
7. spend v.(spant)(人)花费(时间、钱)
```



## 知识拓展

```js
【一般过去时 vs. 主谓双宾】
```

```js
【主谓双宾】
- 双宾语 = 人 + 物
- 哪些及物动词vt.能接双宾语
	vt.能同时接人或物的(tall sb./sth.)
	- send teach lend give ask tell offer
    - cook buy get make write bring show...
    ☆ 能接双宾语，但不一定非要接双宾语
    双宾语动词 + sb. + sth. = 双宾语动词 + sth. +to(表方向）/for(表目的，为了) + sb.
```
